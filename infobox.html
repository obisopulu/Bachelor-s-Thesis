FOUNDER'S STATUE
This statue is located at the center of campus, standing tall and facing the campus main gate.

The Statue is that of the founder of the university; Archbishop Benson Idahosa, who according to the statue is holding a book and a microphone, and is addressing an audience

The Statue was originally placed at bethel (the small field in front of the Faculty of Basic and Applied Science) before it was moved to its corrent possition
